Move in a Sequence
How to Play
×

Move the cow using the buttons and you will see the text in order of the buttons you pushed.

After you have selected the order you want, press 'Move in a Sequence' button to see the cow move.

You'll see your which movement you selected under "What's the Sequence?"

Up
Left
Right
